EFtex Knit Stop KS 103 laser control

Thread break detection and weft control for hosiery machines. The guard uses laser light barriers, which will shut down the machine reliably and rapidly so that losses are kept to a minimum.



  • Features
  • High profitability by impeccable goods on warp knitting machines und double rib looms
  • Up to 8 independent laser light barrier units can be connected, also configurable as 4 twinned pairs
  • 2 channels for monitoring weft threads
  • Modular construction makes adaptation to the machine to be monitored possible
  • Vibration-insensitive laser light barriers with visible, safe, red light (distance up to 25 m)
  • Control your air blower with the KS 103 too
  • Can be connected to a pilot light
