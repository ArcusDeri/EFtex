EFtex Industrielle Messtechnik GmbH was founded by the company’s owner Manfred Eck in 1997. As a young and innovative company, EFtex entirely gears its activities to enhancing quality and reducing costs for the textile producing Excellent knowledge of the sector and know how open up an international market to EFtex in which only a few suppliers have a share.
